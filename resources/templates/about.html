About


This app is my playground for exploring the functionalities of ThreeJS. I have not studied geomatics engineering so this app is not an super accurate 'satellite positioning tool', it's mostly just for fun.


Some known inaccuracies

  • Planets are modeled as spheres and not ellipsoids.
  • The equator and prime meridian are probably not exactly where they should be.
  • The texture applied on the planet sphere may lead to scaling issues regarding the relative sizes of different areas on the planet.

The source code to this app can be found on my github page. Any bugs and problems can be freely posted as issues.
