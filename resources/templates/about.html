About


This project started as a debugging tool for validating my solutions to the Reaktor Orbital Challenge. Then I added some visuals and controls and after that everything got out of hand. It should be noted that I haven't studied geomatics engineering or similar sciences so there might be many inaccuracies with how geographic positions are mapped to the graphical representation of the Earth.


Some known inaccuracies

  • I prioritized cool.
  • Earth is modeled as a sphere and not an ellipsoid.
  • The equator and prime meridian are probably not spot on.
  • The texture applied on the ThreeJS sphere mesh may or may not lead to scaling issues regarding the relative sizes of different areas on the globe.

The source code to this app can be found on my github page.
